CPU Cooler

NZXT Kraken M22 Liquid CPU Cooler

Fan RPM: up to 2000 RPM

Noise Level: 21-36 dB


This CPU Cooler has given me no problems. The temperatures are sub-par, usually keeping my CPU around 50-60°C while idle. On the bright side, it doesn't go up much more while gaming or under load. This could be due to the CPU being from AMD, a company which isn't known to be efficient or run cool. Other than this, the radiator fan has been very quiet, which is nice.

Overall, this cooler has treated me okay, I only wish the temperatures were at least slightly lower.
